The Best Movies According to Tina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Haikyuu!

This is my favourite anime. It's about volleyball, which is my favorite sport. The animation is sooo beautiful!

Arcane

It's a really cool animation series. The characters are from the game League of Legend.

Blue Lock

Another sport anime but this one is about soccer. The themes in Blue Lock are very original and interesting. It is based on the manga with the same name.
